## Day C — Pump and detail focus

| Exercise | Working Sets × Reps | Warm-up Sets | Rest Between Sets | Partial Reps? |
| -------- | ------------------- | ------------ | ----------------- | ------------- |
| Hack squat or leg press | 3 × 10–12 | 10 @ 40% · 8 @ 60% · 5 @ 75% | 2–2.5 min | No — full depth required |
| Close-grip lat pulldown | 3 × 10–12 | 10 @ 50% · 8 @ 70% | 2–2.5 min | No — full stretch essential |
| Machine chest press or dips | 3 × 10–12 | 10 @ 50% · 6 @ 70% | 2–2.5 min | No — especially not on dips |
| Cable lateral raise | 3 × 15–20 | — | 45–60 sec | Yes* — sets 2 & 3, raise to 45° |
| Face pulls | 2 × 15–20 | — | 45–60 sec | Yes* — last set only, halfway pulls (stretched to mid) |
| EZ bar curl | 2 × 10–12 | — | 60–90 sec | Yes* — last set only, bottom-half (stretched position) |
| Triceps pressdown | 2 × 10–12 | — | 60–90 sec | Yes* — last set only, top-half (contracted position) |
| Standing calf raise | 3 × 12–15 | — | 60–75 sec | Yes* — last set only, top-half bounces |
| Hanging leg raise | 2 × 10–15 | — | 45–60 sec | No — full ROM for core |

*Partial reps after completing the minimum target reps. 3–5 half reps max (5–10 for calves). No rest-pause between full and partial reps.

**Cable lateral raise exception:** partials allowed on sets 2 and 3 (not just the last set) — this is the only exercise where this applies, because the rep target is 15–20 and the goal is pure pump.
